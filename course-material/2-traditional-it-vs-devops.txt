Traditional IT
1.) Less Productive
2.) Skill Centric Team
3.) More Time invested in planning

DevOps
1.) More Productive

3.) Smaller and Frequent releases lead to easy scheduling and less time in planning
4.) Frequent releases, with continuous feedback
